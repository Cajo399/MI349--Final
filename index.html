A Little About Myself
Hi there! I'm Caleb, a recent college graduate and user researcher/designer with a passion for creating engaging and accessible user experinces.

I have my B.A. from Michigan State University in Information Science with a focus on Human Computer Interaction; during my time there I was inovled in various research projects and activites related to human-technology interaction, media technology, and data visualization.

When I'm not busy working you can usually find me out enjoying nature, reading a good book, or out on the golf course!
